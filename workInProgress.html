System for Managing Academic Information

For this project I am part of a team of 6 members. We are working on: diagrams, database, backend, frontend. We will use Git for version management. The System will keep track of informaton for each student starting with the first undergraduate year and finishing with the last one or with the last master year. Each faculty may have different specialisations both for the undergraduate and master degrees. A student is enrolled in one of these. Each faculty year will have a contract of studies and a curriculum.
