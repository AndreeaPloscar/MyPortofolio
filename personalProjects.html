Personal Projects

Smart shopping cart - Grabble

Grabble is a smart solution that makes shopping quicker, more efficient and easier. It contains an app developed in Android Studio using Java and a hardware component for the shopping cart. The hardware part constains a nodeMCU module, weight sensors and a RFID reader. The user can choose the cart scanning its QR code and he can add products one by one to the cart by scanning their barcodes. The scale validates the product and the RFID reader detects the RFID safety sticker if the product has one. The system deactivates the stickers when the corresponding products are paid. This project was developed during DpIT program in a team and it was awarded 2nd place after the final presentation.

Bee hive monitoring system - BeeSmart

BeeSmart is an app that makes the work of beekeepers easier by giving them real time information about the state of the hive and showing them statistics about their hives. The project contains an app developed in Android Studio using Java and a hardware part consisting of multiple sensors (weight, temperature, humidity) and a nodeMCU module. This project was developed during DpIT program in a team and it was awarded first place after the final presentation.

App for mental health - Mentable

Mentable is designed to help people take care of their mental health through daily questionaires that can be monitored by a proffesional in this field, puzzles to help the user relax, a calendar to keep track of their activity and a journal. It was developed in a team for the international contest ITnovation.
